Party at the Park

by Wiliam Writer

Residents from the city of Preston, ID gathered together at the city park this past Saturday evening for the annual "Summer Celebration" event. Locals came together to celebrate the first day of summer with a hotdog/hamburger cookout, games, and live music provided by local musicians and bands. The festivities began around 4pm and continued well into the night with the live concert and a surprise visit from "the King" himself, Elvis Presley. Earlier in the evening, there were many other activities for attendees to participate in which included softball games, a fishing competition, a variety of yard games (etc. cornhole, horseshoes), and line dancing lessons to name a few. It was a great time for the townsfolk to come together and celebrate the new season. If you happened to miss this year's wonderful celebration, be sure to mark your calendars and join us next year and check back here at Sunny Day News for the lastest information on upcoming events happening in your area.
