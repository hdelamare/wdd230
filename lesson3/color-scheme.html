Color Scheme

The colors listed below will be my primary and accent colors for most of the site design. The orange-yellow is pulled from the site logo with colors complementing it. All text within the main content will be displayed in either black or white depending on the background color it's placed upon. The primary and secondary colors will be used most often and together throughout the site design, with the two accent colors working to balance out some of the brightness.

Primary: True Blue [#4464AD]
Secondary: Orange Yellow [#E5B012]
Accent #1: Cardinal [#AD343E]
Accent #2: Nickel [#626C66]
