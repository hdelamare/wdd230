Site Purpose

The purpose of our site is to provide our community with the best up-to-date information of everything going on in Southeastern Idaho. We want everyone to feel welcome because we're a community and because we have a little something for everyone. From weather forecasts and the best time to plant to events going on in the area and outdoor recreation ideas. This site not only seeks to provide information for our community, but be a place that can bring our community together by allowing our members to use this site to post/publish their own content. Whether it's a service project that needs volunteers or photos taken in the area, this will allow our site to be a central hub where the community can get the information they need and do it faster. We have it all and our community covered.
