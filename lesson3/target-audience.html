Target Audience

The target audience for this site will cover a wide range of peoples. The site will mostly geared towards those living in Southeastern Idaho. Whether it be men/women, or young/old, we aim to have something for everyone in the area. That being said, however, our best demographic will likely be that of middle-aged to older men and women. People that have more time on their hands and are more interested in being a part of their towns and communities.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: I took some photos at Bear Lake of some wildlife last weekend and want to share them.
  • Scenario 2: The skies are cloudy today... I wonder if it's going to rain soon?
  • Scenario 3: The weather looks bad this morning. What will my commute be like? What are the current road conditions?
  • Scenario 4: I'm hosting a local event and want to get the word out to as many people as possible in the area.
  • Scenario 5: We're planning a campout this weekend. I should check the weather forecast so that way we know what to expect.
