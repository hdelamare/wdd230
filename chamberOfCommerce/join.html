Membership Level Benefits

Non-profit:

Best for solopreneurs and individuals seeking networking access through the Chamber.

  • Business name listed in webite directory
  • Eligible to post events on Chamber's Event Calendar
  • Membership certificate, and business window sticker
  • Access to monthly newsletter

Bronze:

Best for small to medium-sized businesses.

Additional Benefits Include...
  • Listing on website directory including logo and information
  • Ribbon Cutting/Grand Opening event promotion
  • Eligible for ads in the newsletter
  • Eligible to list job openings on Chamber website

Silver:

Best for medium to large-sized businesses.

Additional Benefits Include...
  • Featured once annually on Chamber social media pages
  • Free booth provided at all Chamber events
  • Access to two free one-time use emails using our community mailing list for marketing
  • Tickets to annual Awards Event

Gold:

Best for small to medium-sized businesses.

Additional Benefits Include...
  • Boosted social media features
  • Logo feautured throughout Chamber website
  • Annual feature in Chamber newsletter
  • Annual meeting with Board of Directors
