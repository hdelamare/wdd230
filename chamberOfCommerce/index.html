This Month's Featured Businesses

BigBoy's is a staple in our community. They opened all the way back in 1963 and they're still going strong today. Offering standard American-fare with classic flat-top smashed burgers and the best onion rings in town! Give 'em a try today!

Hawaiian Bros is a more recent local eatery that opened up shop back in 2018, but has quickly boomed in popularity allowing for multiple locations around the KC area. Offering traditional Hawaiian meals with HUGE portions, for a reasonable price. If you've ever wanted to travel to Hawaii, but on a budget, stop by your local Hawaiian Bros for that authentic Hawaii flavor and vibes!

Tay's has been around for a few years, since 2015, but they're showing no signs of slowing down through offering Kansas City one of the best burgers you'll ever have! Winner five years in a row of "KC's Best Burger" Award, that's probably because they only use high-quality, grass-fed Angus beef, and it's truly a difference you can taste. Stop by today to get your own 5 time winner burger, and don't forget the housemade Sriracha Mayo!
